These files are for OpenTESArena. They are updated every once in a while.

Project website:
- https://github.com/afritz1/OpenTESArena

Running the executable:
1. Copy the "ARENA" floppy disk folder into data/, or change "ArenaPath" in options.txt to point to an existing location on your computer.
2. Change "Soundfont" in options.txt to point to a soundfont .cfg on your computer (i.e., data/eawpats/timidity.cfg).
